Global Climate Policy

Global Climate Policy and Advocacy centers on collaborative international agreements, notably the Paris Agreement, to tackle climate change. These agreements serve as frameworks for nations to commit to emissions reductions and climate resilience efforts.
In tandem, advocacy efforts by organizations and activists push for more ambitious climate action. They raise awareness, lobby governments, and promote sustainable practices in various sectors.

Despite progress, challenges like political barriers and insufficient funding persist, underscoring the ongoing need for robust advocacy and policy coordination to combat climate change effectively.
global climate policy and advocacy represent the international steps that have addressed the current issue of climate change. Paris agreement is one of well known landmark occasion which has depicted international level negotiations to enforce domestic and regional based regulations.

furthermore, it has highlighted the profiles of climate activists and organizations that raises their voice to change the notions of our community for strong foundational decisions on climate change.
